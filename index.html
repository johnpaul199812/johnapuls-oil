Johnapul's Oil was born from a simple belief: every Nigerian kitchen deserves access to pure, high-quality palm oil at a fair price.

We work directly with local palm farmers, ensuring sustainable sourcing while maintaining the traditional processing methods that give our oil its distinctive rich color and authentic flavor.
